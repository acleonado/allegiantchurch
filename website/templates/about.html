Welcome to The Allegiant Christian Church!

We are a small non-denominational Christian Church who are mostly Filipinos but we're open to anyone who wants to worship and discover the Lord in their lives. We are a church who believes in Jesus Christ and are committed to walk with you as you learn how He can work and change your lives. We encourage each and every one of you to invite your friends and families to praise and worship the Lord with us and join us in our journey.

Let us follow Jesus

Let us praise and worship His goodness

Let us remember He is our Army

Let us not forget He is bigger than your worries

And thank Him for all that He has done for us
